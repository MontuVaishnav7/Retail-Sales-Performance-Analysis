# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

🚀 End-to-End Data Analytics Project | Python + MySQL + Power BI | 9,994 Transactions | $2.3M Sales Analysis

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify revenue trends, profitability drivers, customer behavior patterns, product performance, and regional sales opportunities.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end Data Analytics workflow using Python, MySQL, and Power BI.

---

## Business Objective

The goal of this project is to answer critical business questions:

* Which categories generate the highest sales and profit?
* Which products contribute most to revenue?
* Which customer segments are most valuable?
* How do discounts affect profitability?
* Which regions offer growth opportunities?

## Key Insights

### Technology Category Leads Performance

Technology generated the highest sales and profit contribution.

### Consumer Segment Drives Revenue

Consumer customers contributed the largest share of overall revenue.

### West Region Performs Best

The West region generated the highest sales and profitability.

### Discounts Reduce Profitability

Higher discounts were associated with lower profit margins.

### Top Products Drive Revenue Growth

Products such as Canon imageCLASS significantly contributed to overall sales.
